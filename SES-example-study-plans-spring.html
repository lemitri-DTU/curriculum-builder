Example Study Plans — MSc Sustainable Energy Systems (Spring Start)

DTU 2025 Curriculum · 10 student profiles · Thesis (30 ECTS) assumed in Fall of Year 2 unless noted

These ten example plans show how different student profiles might structure their 120 ECTS MSc programme (90 ECTS courses + 30 ECTS thesis) when starting in Spring. Each plan satisfies all pool requirements for its chosen specialization. Polytechnic foundation courses and Innovation II courses are included. Where noted, 5 ECTS may be scheduled alongside the thesis. The Study Plan Builder tool lets you replicate and customize these plans interactively. Course colors match the requirement pools shown in the builder for each specialization.
1

Digital Energy Systems
Spring-start variant. Model-based ML and Scientific Programming in Spring Y1 build the analytical toolkit, then the full digital stack — Computational Tools for Data Science, ML for Energy Systems, and Optimization in Power Systems — lands in Fall. Year 2 adds Advanced ML in Spring, with Intelligent Systems and Applied AI for Entrepreneurs alongside the thesis for a complete data-to-deployment skillset.
