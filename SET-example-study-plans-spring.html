Example Study Plans — MSc Sustainable Energy Technologies (Spring Start)

DTU 2025 Curriculum · 10 student profiles · Thesis (30 ECTS) assumed in Fall of Year 2 (with thesis)

These ten example plans show how different student profiles might structure their 120 ECTS MSc programme (90 ECTS courses + 30 ECTS thesis) when starting in Spring. Each plan satisfies all pool requirements for its chosen specialization. Polytechnic foundation and Innovation II courses are included. Course colors match the requirement pools shown in the builder for each specialization. The Study Plan Builder tool lets you replicate and customize these plans interactively.
